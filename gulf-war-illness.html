Gulf War Illness (GWI) — automated daily PubMed feed of peer-reviewed research.

— studies

Automated daily feed of peer-reviewed studies on Gulf War Illness (GWI), also known as Gulf War syndrome or Gulf War veterans illness, affecting veterans of the 1990–1991 Persian Gulf War. GWI is a chronic multi-symptom illness with neurological, immune, and autonomic features including fatigue, cognitive impairment, musculoskeletal pain, and gastrointestinal symptoms. It is considered a post-toxic/post-infectious chronic illness with overlapping features with ME/CFS and other post-viral syndromes.
