## Claims deliberately not shipped

Per the brief's core rule ("when in doubt between shipping an unverified claim and cutting it, cut it"), the following were investigated but not included as standalone cited claims:

1. Succinate/itaconate shunt operating specifically in Long COVID/ME-CFS (no disease-specific primary source found — described as unconfirmed in mechanism prose only).
2. Baseline NAD⁺ depletion in Long COVID/ME-CFS patients (only treatment-response trials found, not baseline-measurement studies).
3. A completed negative/null trial of anticoagulant treatment for fibrinaloid microclots (none found — only non-peer-reviewed clinical pushback, which lacks a resolvable identifier).
4. General disease-specific peripheral oxidative-stress-marker studies in ME/CFS beyond the one CNS glutathione study (real papers reportedly exist but no specific identifier was resolved and confirmed in this session for any single one).
