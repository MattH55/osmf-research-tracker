Cross-Condition Biomarker Overlap

These conditions share clinical phenotypes and convergent biomarker domains. Entries marked "overlap" in individual atlases indicate shared literature findings.

Autonomic / HRV

Reduced heart rate variability and orthostatic intolerance appear across ME/CFS, Long COVID, PACVS, Lyme (PTLDS), and GWI.

Mitochondrial / Metabolic

Acylcarnitines, lactate, ATP deficit, and amino acid depletion (serine, sarcosine) are reported in ME/CFS, Long COVID, and GWI cohorts.

GPCR Autoantibodies

β-adrenergic and muscarinic receptor autoantibodies link dysautonomia phenotypes in ME/CFS, Long COVID, PACVS, and GWI research.
