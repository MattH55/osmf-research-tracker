Peer-reviewed studies on Chronic Lyme disease and PTLDS (Post-Treatment Lyme Disease Syndrome), also called post-Lyme syndrome or chronic Lyme syndrome. PTLDS describes persistent symptoms — fatigue, pain, cognitive difficulties — lasting months after antibiotic treatment of Borrelia burgdorferi infection. Research spans immune dysregulation, bacterial persistence, autoimmunity, and treatment trials for tick-borne disease sequelae.
