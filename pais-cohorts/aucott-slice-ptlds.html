Johns Hopkins SLICE early-Lyme cohort (Aucott/Rebman)

source record (JSON) · schema v2.0.0 · CC BY 4.0

Design notes & limitations. Prospective inception cohort of ideally-treated early Lyme disease with a non-Lyme comparison group and a bio-repository, designed specifically to estimate the incidence of post-treatment Lyme disease. Functionally-impairing persistent symptoms occurred in 14% of treated patients vs 4% of controls, giving a rigorous denominator for PTLD that most Lyme literature lacks.
