Bergen COVID-19 home-isolated cohort (Blomberg)

source record (JSON) · licensed CC BY 4.0

Design notes & limitations. Notable because it captured a near-complete community denominator (82% of local cases) and included predominantly mild, home-isolated patients, showing that persistent symptoms at 6 months affect even young adults with mild acute disease. No unexposed comparator.
