Toronto Chronic Post-SARS Cohort (Moldofsky)

source record (JSON) · licensed CC BY 4.0

Design notes & limitations. Deliberately included as a methodologically weak but historically important study: small (22 cases, 7 controls), cross-sectional case-control with a convenience control group, but the first to characterize an ME/CFS/fibromyalgia-like chronic syndrome after a coronavirus — an early precedent for Long COVID.
