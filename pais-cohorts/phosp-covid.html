PHOSP-COVID (UK post-hospitalisation COVID-19)

source record (JSON) · licensed CC BY 4.0

Design notes & limitations. Large multicentre prospective cohort of hospitalised COVID-19 survivors with an active biobank and deep phenotyping/inflammation profiling. Full recovery was reported by only about a quarter of participants and did not improve meaningfully between 5 months and 1 year. No unexposed comparison group.
