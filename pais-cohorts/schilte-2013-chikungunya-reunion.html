Réunion chikungunya viremic inception cohort (Schilte)

source record (JSON) · licensed CC BY 4.0

Design notes & limitations. Inception cohort defined by acute RT-PCR viraemia (not just serology), with banked acute-phase specimens and 3-year follow-up of chronic arthralgia. A rarer, stronger complement to the serology-ascertained TELECHIK cohort on the same outbreak.
