Right to Try Registry

Diseases without FDA-approved therapies where investigational drugs may be accessed outside clinical trials under right-to-try frameworks.

About This Registry

Current Status: This page displays all 106 disease profiles in the RepurpOS database, sorted by number of known therapeutic agents (fewest first). It is a work-in-progress toward a full Right-to-Try registry.

Final Design: When complete, this registry will show only diseases without FDA-approved disease-modifying therapy, following the framework in BUILD_SPEC.md (stages 0–8). It will distinguish federal right-to-try eligibility (21 U.S.C. § 360bbb-0a) from Montana SB 535 access, and will flag the critical traps: drug-level (not indication-level) eligibility, ex-US approval non-disqualification, and Phase 1 population risk calibration.

Data Basis: Open Targets Platform, ClinicalTrials.gov, FDA Orange/Purple Books, and OSMF curation. Each disease profile includes molecular targets, clinical biomarkers, and ranked investigational agents.
