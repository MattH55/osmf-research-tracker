PAIS Cohort Database

A catalogue of cohorts (study populations followed for post-acute infection syndrome outcomes), not patients and not publications. The unit of record is the cohort, so that one cohort's many papers do not overstate how much independent evidence exists. The point of the database is to make the design of each study — denominator, controls, timepoints, specimens — machine-readable, so the incomparability of the literature and its gaps become visible.

21 cohorts · seed release · every field human-verified against source · data licensed CC BY 4.0 · schema · spec

This is a seed of 21 design-verified cohorts, not a complete census. It deliberately mixes strong designs (prospective inception cohorts with unexposed controls) and weak ones (self-selected online surveys) so the gap matrices below are honest. Contributions are made as pull requests; schema validation runs in CI and blocks merges that break it. No pooled estimates or quality scores are computed — filter the design fields and judge for yourself.
