An automated collection of peer-reviewed publications on POTS (Postural Orthostatic Tachycardia Syndrome) and related dysautonomia. POTS is a form of autonomic dysfunction characterized by an excessive heart rate increase on standing, often with dizziness, fatigue, brain fog, exercise intolerance, and syncope or pre-syncope. It commonly follows viral infection, including SARS-CoV-2 and COVID-19 vaccination, and frequently overlaps with Long COVID, PACVS, and ME/CFS phenotypes.
