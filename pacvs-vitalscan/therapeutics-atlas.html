Evidence limitations

This atlas synthesises published evidence as of July 2026. Evidence tiers reflect the best available published evidence per agent-condition pair, not necessarily sufficient evidence for clinical use. T1 RCT data in Long COVID does not always indicate approved indications. All treatment decisions should be made in consultation with qualified healthcare professionals. For conditions like MCAS and ME/CFS where placebo-controlled RCT data is sparse, T2 evidence extrapolated from related conditions forms the evidence base. Trial data should be checked for current status on ClinicalTrials.gov.
