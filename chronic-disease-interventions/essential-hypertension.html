Remission & chronicity

Spontaneous remission
Not well-characterized as population baseline; routinely framed as irreversible in clinical culture despite contrary trial evidence
Best-intervention remission
Up to 93% had medications discontinued or reduced with intensive residential lifestyle intervention; DASH + weight loss reduced guideline-based medication indication from 51% to 18%; Lancet eClinicalMedicine 2024 meta-analysis confirms lifestyle superiority in motivated patients
Gap size
Large — primarily a framing and practice gap
Primary barrier
framing/practice
The 'requires lifelong medication' framing is contradicted by multiple trials when intensive lifestyle intervention is actually delivered; the gap is delivery infrastructure, not biology
