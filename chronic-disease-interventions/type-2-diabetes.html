Remission & chronicity

Spontaneous remission
1.6-1.7% at 7 years on standard care (population-level cohorts UK and US)
Best-intervention remission
46-58% at 5 years post-bariatric surgery; up to 86% at 1 year with >15% total weight loss via intensive dietary intervention (DiRECT trial: 36% at 2 years)
Gap size
Very large
Primary barrier
practice/access
Remission is biologically achievable and demonstrated in multiple large RCTs but almost never offered as the primary clinical strategy; default is indefinite pharmacotherapy escalation
