An automated collection of peer-reviewed publications on ME/CFS (Myalgic Encephalomyelitis / Chronic Fatigue Syndrome), also called Systemic Exertion Intolerance Disease (SEID) or chronic fatigue syndrome (CFS). ME/CFS is a serious neuroimmune condition characterized by post-exertional malaise (PEM), unrefreshing sleep, orthostatic intolerance, and cognitive impairment ("brain fog"). It frequently emerges after viral infection and shows substantial mechanistic overlap with Long COVID and PACVS.
