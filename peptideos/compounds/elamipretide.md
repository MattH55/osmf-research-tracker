# Elamipretide (Investigational)

## MANDATORY STANDING NOTICE

**This compound is investigational. It is not approved in any jurisdiction. There is no lawful supply. Any material sold under this name is of unverified origin and neither manufactured nor tested to pharmaceutical standards.**

---

Mitochondrial-targeting peptide in Phase 2/3 development (sponsor: Stealth BioTherapeutics) for rare mitochondrial diseases.

**Mechanism:** Targets inner mitochondrial membrane; proposed to restore cardiolipin remodeling and electron transport chain function (mechanism not fully characterized in humans).

## Evidence Summary

### Barth Syndrome (E3)
Phase 2 open-label (N~30): Modest LVEF improvement (+5–10%) and exercise tolerance in cardiolipinopathy. Orphan Drug designated. Phase 3 planned.

### Leigh Syndrome (E4)
Case reports and exploratory Phase 2 data; unproven clinical benefit.

## Safety Profile (S2)

Injection site reactions (~10%, transient). No serious adverse events reported in limited Phase 2 populations.

## Trial Status

- Barth Syndrome Phase 3: Planned (FDA orphan pathway guidance)
- Leigh Syndrome Phase 2: Exploratory, ongoing

Expected approval timeline: Unknown (depends on Phase 3 initiation and enrollment of ultra-rare population).

## What Is Unknown

1. **Phase 3 efficacy in Barth Syndrome.** Trials not yet initiated; ultra-rare population (recruitment challenges)
2. **Long-term cardiac benefit.** Phase 2 max 12 weeks; durability unknown
3. **Mechanism in humans.** Cardiolipin remodeling and mitochondrial function not directly measured in Phase 2
4. **Efficacy in other mitochondrial disorders.** No human data; biologically plausible but unproven
