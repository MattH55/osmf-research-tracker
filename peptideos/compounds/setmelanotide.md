# Setmelanotide (Imcivree)

MC4R agonist approved for rare genetic obesity caused by POMC, PCSK1, or LEPR pathway deficiency.

**Mechanism:** MC4R agonism → activation of POMC neurons → appetite suppression.

## Evidence Summary

### Genetic Obesity (POMC/PCSK1/LEPR Deficiency) (E1)
Phase 3 RCT (N=61): 10.2% weight loss vs. 2.1% placebo. First FDA-approved therapy for monogenic obesity.

## Safety Profile (S3)

Skin darkening (~50%, cosmetic, reversible), nausea (~10%), rare penile fibrosis/Peyronie's-like changes (~2 cases).
