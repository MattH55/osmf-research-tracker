# Tesamorelin (Egrifta)

GHRH agonist approved for HIV-associated lipodystrophy.

**Mechanism:** GHRH-R agonism → GH secretion → visceral fat lipolysis.

## Evidence Summary

20% reduction in visceral adiposity vs. 5% placebo.

## Safety Profile (S2)

Hyperglycemia (~10%), carpal tunnel syndrome (~5%), theoretical malignancy risk (GH mechanism).
