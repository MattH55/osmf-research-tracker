# Octreotide (Sandostatin)

## Overview

Octreotide is a long-acting somatostatin analog, approved for acromegaly and carcinoid syndrome. It is an 8-amino-acid cyclic peptide.

**Mechanism:** Somatostatin receptor agonism (SSTR2, SSTR5) → inhibition of GH and IGF-1 secretion (acromegaly); inhibition of hormone secretion from neuroendocrine tumors (carcinoid syndrome).

## Evidence Summary

### Acromegaly (E1)
Phase 3 trials: GH suppression to <2.5 mIU/L in 60–75%. Symptom improvement in acromegalic features, carpal tunnel.

### Carcinoid Syndrome (E1)
Multiple trials: 70–85% reduction in carcinoid flushing and diarrhea.

## Safety Profile (S2)

Gallstone formation (~25% at 5+ years), hyperglycemia (~5%), GI upset.

## What Is Unknown

1. Long-term gallstone intervention strategies
2. Optimal dosing for GH control vs. side effects
