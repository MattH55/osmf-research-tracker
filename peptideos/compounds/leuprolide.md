# Leuprolide (Lupron)

GnRH agonist approved for advanced prostate cancer, endometriosis, and uterine fibroids.

**Mechanism:** GnRH-R agonism → initial FSH/LH surge, then downregulation → testosterone suppression to castrate levels (<20 ng/dL).

## Evidence Summary

Prostate cancer: Testosterone suppression >95%, equivalent to surgical castration. Standard-of-care.

## Safety Profile (S2)

Hot flashes (80%), sexual dysfunction, tumor flare (initial testosterone surge). Cardiovascular effects from long-term testosterone suppression.
