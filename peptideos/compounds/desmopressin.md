# Desmopressin (DDAVP)

## Overview

Desmopressin is a synthetic vasopressin (AVP) analog, approved for central diabetes insipidus and nocturnal enuresis.

**Mechanism:** V2 receptor agonism in kidney collecting duct → aquaporin-2 insertion → increased water reabsorption.

## Evidence Summary

### Diabetes Insipidus (E1)
85–95% control of polyuria/polydipsia.

### Nocturnal Enuresis (E1)
70–80% reduction in wet nights vs. 20% placebo.

## Safety Profile (S2)

Hyponatremia with overdose (~1–3% oral); generally safe. Headache (~5%).

## What Is Unknown

1. Hyponatremia patient stratification
2. Long-term safety >5 years
