# Mazdutide (Investigational)

## MANDATORY STANDING NOTICE

**This compound is investigational. It is not approved in any jurisdiction. There is no lawful supply. Any material sold under this name is of unverified origin and neither manufactured nor tested to pharmaceutical standards.**

---

GLP-1/GCG dual agonist in early Phase 1b/2a development (sponsor: Apeiron Therapeutics).

**Mechanism:** GLP-1 + GCG receptor co-agonism → glucose-dependent insulin secretion + hepatic glucose output suppression.

## Evidence Summary

### Type 2 Diabetes (E4)
Early Phase 1b/2a; no reported efficacy or safety data.

### Weight Loss (E8)
No human data; preclinical stage only.

## Safety Profile (S4—High Uncertainty)

No human safety data. Mechanism suggests dual GLP-1 (nausea, pancreatitis) + GCG (biliary, hepatic glucose suppression) risks.

## Trial Status

- Phase 1b/2a T2DM: Ongoing (enrollment/completion status unknown)

No announced approval pathway or timeline.

## What Is Unknown

1. **Human efficacy.** No Phase 1b/2a data reported yet
2. **Human safety.** Early-stage; no human exposure data published
3. **Tolerability.** Dual mechanism nausea and GCG signals unknown
4. **Development timeline.** No regulatory guidance from sponsor
