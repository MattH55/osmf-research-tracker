This page shows an automated selection of peer-reviewed publications related to Post-Acute COVID-19 Vaccination Syndrome (PACVS). Also known as post-vaccination syndrome, COVID vaccine injury, and persistent post-vaccination syndrome, PACVS encompasses prolonged symptoms following COVID-19 vaccination, including fatigue, post-exertional malaise, autonomic dysfunction (POTS, dysautonomia), small fiber neuropathy, mast cell activation syndrome (MCAS), and immune dysregulation. It shares mechanistic overlap with Long COVID/PASC and ME/CFS.
